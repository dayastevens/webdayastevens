About Me

I am a freelance science writer and journalist based in Philadelphia. I write about research, nature, and the people behind it. I love stories that make science feel human.

My work has appeared in The Philadelphia Citizen and Colorado Green News. I am currently working in neuromuscular clinical research at the University of Pennsylvania, but when I'm not writing or working you can find me hiking, camping, thrifting, or with my dog :)
